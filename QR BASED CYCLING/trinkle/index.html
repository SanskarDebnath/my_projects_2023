Paddle Towards A Carbon Free Future

Trinkle is a Tripura-based startup committed to providing sustainable and carbon-free public transportation solutions through app-based bicycle sharing. Our mission is to revolutionize the way people commute by offering an eco-friendly alternative that reduces traffic congestion and promotes healthier lifestyles. With our user-friendly mobile application, we strive to create a seamless experience for our customers while making a positive impact on the environment.

How To trinkle ?

Using trinkle app is as easy as it can get. Just follow the three steps "Locate, Scan, Ride"

Get Our App

Download the trinkle app from google play store. Also available for IOS on app store.

Locate Our Hub

Open the trinkle app and automatically locate the nearest trinkle hub near you.

Scan QR To Use

Scan the QR code on the available trinkle bike to start your journey.

Our Objective

At our cycling service in Tripura, our primary objective is to reduce carbon emissions by promoting the use of bicycles for short-distance travel. We believe that our efforts will contribute to creating a green economy by reducing the carbon footprint and preserving the environment for future generations.We aim to provide an affordable and convenient alternative to traditional modes of transportation while promoting a healthy lifestyle. By reducing reliance on fossil fuels, we hope to contribute to a sustainable future and create a positive impact on the local community and the planet.Our mission is to create a culture of eco-consciousness and sustainability by encouraging people to make environmentally responsible choices. We believe that through our efforts, we can build a better tomorrow for everyone, one pedal at a time. Join us in our journey towards a greener, more sustainable future.
